FAQ

What is Intersolar?

Intersolar is a digital universe of solar systems with planets and suns represented as NFTs on the Solana blockchain.

What does NFT mean?

NFT stands for Non-fungible token. Our NFTs are stored on the Solana blockchain to provide proof of ownership. The NFT images itself are stored permanently on the permaweb using the Arweave Blockchain.

How can I buy a planet / NFT?

Purchasing a planet is super easy. Connect your Solana wallet and visit our minting site.

Step-By-Step Guide
  1. Download the Phantom wallet extension for your browser (Google Chrome recommended)
  2. Buy Solana (SOL) on an exchange like Coinbase etc. and send it to your Phantom wallet public Address.
  3. Connect your Phantom wallet to our minting site by clicking on the Connect Wallet button.
  4. Click on Mint.

Where can I buy a planet?

New planets are always available via our minting site. You can also trade them on various Solana marketplaces with other planet owners.

How many traits does a planet have?

Planets can have up to 5 traits (surroundings, surface, natural satellites, rings, and events). All trait variants have different levels of rareness, so there will be combinations that are super rare.

Do planets have secondary sale royalties?

Yes, royalties are set at 7.5% for secondary sales.

Am I the owner of the planet after buying?

Yes, full ownership of the token is transferred to the buyer after sale.
